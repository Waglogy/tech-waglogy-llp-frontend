## Bonus — Evergreen post template (use whenever)

**Type:** Update
**Body:**

> [HOOK — single line, question or stat]
>
> [BODY — 3-5 lines explaining the situation, why it matters to a Sikkim/NE business audience]
>
> [LIST — 3-5 bullets of practical takeaways or what you offer]
>
> [CTA — single line directing to your contact / blog / specific page]

Pattern your future posts on this. Keep it under 1,200 chars to stay scannable. Always include one image. Never re-use the same image twice in 30 days — Google notices.

---

## Posting cadence — make it a recurring task

- **Weekly** — One post every Monday morning. Set a recurring calendar reminder.
- **Vary post types** — Don't post 4 offers in a row. Cycle Update → Offer → Update → Update.
- **Always include a CTA button** — GBP posts without CTAs get ~40% less click-through.
- **Track in Insights** — note which posts get most clicks. Double down on those formats.

---

## What NOT to do

- Don't post the same content twice within 90 days
- Don't post text-only — every post needs an image
- Don't use hashtags (GBP doesn't index them)
- Don't use clickbait (Google demotes posts flagged as misleading)
- Don't link to a 404 or to a page that takes >3 seconds to load on mobile
