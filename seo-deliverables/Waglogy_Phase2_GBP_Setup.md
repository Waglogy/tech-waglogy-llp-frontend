## 1. Claim or create the listing (15 min)

1. Go to **business.google.com** and sign in with the Google account you want associated with the business (use the company email if possible — `contact@waglogy.in`).
2. Search for "Tech Waglogy LLP". If a listing exists (often auto-generated by Google from public data), click **Claim this business**. If not, click **Add your business to Google**.
3. Verification options: **postcard** (5–14 days) is most common for India. Phone or email verification appears for some businesses — pick the fastest available. Do not skip verification — unverified profiles do not appear in the Map Pack.
4. While the postcard is in transit, complete every other field below — Google starts ranking the listing the moment it's verified, so the more complete it is, the faster it climbs.

## 9. Common mistakes to avoid

- **Wrong primary category** — "Software company" sounds right but "Website designer" actually ranks better for the queries that matter to you
- **Adding fake reviews** — Google detects this fast and can suspend the listing permanently
- **Setting it once and forgetting** — GBP is a living surface; activity matters more than initial setup
- **Using the personal email as owner** — use `contact@waglogy.in` so any team member can manage it
- **Ignoring the Q&A section** — anyone can ask a question on your listing. Monitor and answer publicly within 24 hours
